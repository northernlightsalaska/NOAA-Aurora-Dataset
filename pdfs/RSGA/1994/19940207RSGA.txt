NORMAL LEVEL.

SHOULD BECOME PREDOMINANTLY ACTIVE FOR THE NEXT THREE DAYS.

MID LATITUDES AND ISOLATED MAJOR TO SEVERE STORM PERIODS
ARE POSSIBLE FOR HIGH LATITUDES.

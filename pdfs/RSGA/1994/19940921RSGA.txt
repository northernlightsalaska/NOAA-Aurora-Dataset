JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 264 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 21 SEP 1994
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 20/2100Z
TO 21/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY CONTINUED AT A VERY LOW LEVEL.
THE SUN WAS AGAIN VOID OF SPOTS DURING THE PERIOD.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY SHOULD CONTINUE
AT A VERY LOW LEVEL FOR THE MAJORITY OF THE NEXT THREE DAYS.
ON 24 SEP, OLD REGION 7773 SHOULD BEGIN TO REAPPEAR AT THE
EAST LIMB. THIS REGION PRODUCED 2 M-CLASS AND 16 C-CLASS
FLARES LAST ROTATION BUT IS NOT EXPECTED TO BE AS ACTIVE
THIS ROTATION.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 20/2100Z TO 21/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET. ENERGETIC ELECTRON FLUXES
RANGED BETWEEN NORMAL AND HIGH LEVELS. IT APPEARS THE
ENHANCEMENT IN THESE PARTICLES WHICH BEGAN ON 08 SEP IS
FINALLY BEGINNING TO WANE.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD
SHOULD BE QUIET TO SLIGHTLY UNSETTLED FOR THE NEXT THREE
DAYS.

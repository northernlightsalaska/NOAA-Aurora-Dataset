IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 27/2100Z
TO 28/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 7654 (N08W90)
PRODUCED AN M1/OF LIMB FLARE AT 1704Z. THIS EVENT REGISTERED A
SECONDARY MAX OF M1 AT 1852Z. POST-FLARE LOOPS WERE SEEN TO
FOLLOW THIS ACTIVITY. BRIGHT SURGING WAS COMMONPLACE ON THE WEST
LIMB ALL DAY. ELSEWHERE, NEW REGION 7662 (S15E36) EMERGED, AND
POINT BRIGHTENINGS WERE SEEN IN AN AS YET UNNUMBERED REGION
NOW POSITIONED NEAR S08E79. AN EIGHT DEGREE-LONG SECTION OF
THE LARGE N-S FILAMENT LOCATED NEAR DISK CENTER DISAPPEARED
BY MIDWAY THROUGH THE INTERVAL.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE. REGION 7654 IS CAPABLE OF YET ANOTHER M-CLASS
FLARE AS A PARTING SHOT.

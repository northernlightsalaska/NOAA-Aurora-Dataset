THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS UNSETTLED TO MINOR STORM AT MIDDLE
LATITUDES, WHILE HIGH LATITUDES WERE EVEN MORE DISTURBED AT
TIMES. THE GREATER THAN 2 MEV ELECTRON FLUX WAS AT HIGH
LEVELS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE THROUGHOUT THE PERIOD AS
THE CURRENT DISTURBANCE WANES. HOWEVER, NIGHTTIME SUBSTORMS
ARE DISTINCTLY POSSIBLE OVER THE NEXT 2 DAYS.

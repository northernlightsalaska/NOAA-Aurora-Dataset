IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 01/2100Z
TO 02/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. TWO C-CLASS FLARES
OCCURRED. THE FIRST WAS AN OPTICALLY UNCORRELATED C2 AT
02/1652Z. THE OTHER, ALSO C2, WAS OBSERVED AT THE EAST LIMB
(NEAR S09) AT 02/2002Z. REGION 7773 (S07E35) REMAINS THE LARGEST
AND MOST COMPLEX GROUP ON THE VISIBLE DISK BUT HAS NOT
PRODUCED SIGNIFICANT FLARES SINCE YESTERDAY. A WEAK NOISE STORM
AT 245 MHZ HAS BEEN IN PROGRESS FOR MOST OF THE DAY. THREE
REGIONS WERE NUMBERED: 7774 (N11E47), 7775 (N14E73), AND 7776
(S07E67).

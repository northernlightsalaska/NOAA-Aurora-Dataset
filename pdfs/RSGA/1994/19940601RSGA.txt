HIGH LEVELS OVER THE PAST 24 HOURS.

OF THE FORECAST PERIOD, THEN AT MOSTLY ACTIVE LEVELS. HIGH
LATITUDE STATIONS SHOULD EXPECT PERIODS OF MAJOR TO SEVERE
STORMING DURING LOCAL NIGHTTIME.

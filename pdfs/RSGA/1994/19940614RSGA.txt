HIGH LATITUDES FROM 14/0900-1800Z.

EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY UNSETTLED UNTIL TOMORROW WHEN A DSF
RELATED DISTURBANCE COULD INCREASE ACTIVITY TO MINOR STORM
LEVELS.

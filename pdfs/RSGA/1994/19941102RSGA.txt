THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD RANGED FROM QUIET TO ACTIVE AT MOST
STATIONS. SOME HIGH LATITUDE LOCATIONS EXPERIENCED STORM
CONDITIONS FROM 02/0900Z TO 1500Z. THE GREATER THAN 2 MEV
ELECTRON FLUX WAS HIGH DURING THE PAST 24 HOURS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE WITH ISOLATED STORM
CONDITIONS AT SOME HIGH LATITUDE LOCATIONS. THE FIELD SHOULD
BECOME QUIET TO UNSETTLED BY THE THIRD DAY.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 20/2100Z
TO 21/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS HIGH.  REGION 5890 (S11E12)
PRODUCED A SINGLE M FLARE DURING THE PAST DAY, AN M6/1N AT
21/0447Z.  THIS REGION HAS OTHERWISE BEEN RELATIVELY QUIET
AND LITTLE CHANGED.  A SECOND M CLASS FLARE OCCURRED IN
REGION 5892 (S27E32), AN M1/SF AT 21/1635Z.  THIS REGION
IS NOT LARGE IN WHITE LIGHT BUT DOES HAVE SOME MAGNETIC
COMPLEXITY APPARENT IN H-ALPHA.  REGION 5900 (S08E67) APPEARS
TO BE A FAIRLY LARGE AND SOMEWHAT COMPLEX REGION, BUT HAS BEEN
RELATIVELY QUIET SINCE ITS ARRIVAL ON THE DISK.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE TO HIGH.  M CLASS ACTIVITY IS POSSIBLE IN REGIONS
5882 (N20W53), 5890, 5892, AND 5900.

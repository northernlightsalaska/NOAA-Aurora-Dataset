FROM SPACE ENVIRONMENT SERVICES CENTER, BOULDER, COLORADO
SDF NUMBER 087
JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY.
ISSUED 2200Z 28 MAR 1990
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 27/2100Z
TO 28/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5988 (S06W37)
PRODUCED AN M4/2N FLARE THAT ATTAINED MAXIMUM AT 0751Z. THIS
LONG-LIVED EVENT INCLUDED A RADIO BURST OF 290 FLUX UNITS AT
10 CM AND OTHER SIMILAR SIZED BURSTS ACROSS THE SPECTRUM. THE
EVENT WAS DEVOID OF TYPES II OR IV ACTIVITY. STRUCTURALLY
THE REGION HAS SHOWN LITTLE CHANGE DURING THE INTERVAL. OTHER
SIGNIFICANT ACTIVITY INCLUDED AN ERUPTIVE PROMINENCE NEAR N56
W90 AND A BRIGHT SURGE NEAR N17W90. ONE NEW REGION WAS
ASSIGNED, 5998 (S16E14).
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. BOTH REGIONS 5988 AND 5984 (S13W70) HAVE SHOWN
THE ABILITY TO PRODUCE M-LEVEL ACTIVITY. ALSO REGION 5991
(N23W67) MAY ALSO CONTRIBUTE AN ISOLATED M-CLASS EVENT. IT
PRODUCED FREQUENT SUBFLARES AS IT DOUBLED IN SIZE DURING THE
INTERVAL.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 27/2100Z TO 28/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE
LEVELS FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS. A VERY SMALL ENHANCEMENT IN
THE GREATER THAN 10 MEV PROTON FLUX BEGAN ABOUT 1500Z.
THIS INCREASE, THOUGHT TO BE A RESULT OF THE M4/2N FLARE,
IS AT 2 PFU AT THIS WRITING.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO PERSIST AT UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE LEVELS THE NEXT
THREE DAYS. THE PROTON ENHANCEMENT SHOULD ABATE DURING THE
NEXT 24 HOURS.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 07/2100Z
TO 08/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW.  THE LARGEST FLARE OF THE
PERIOD WAS A C7/SF FROM REGION 6402 (S11W40) AT 0648Z.  REGION
6398 (N15W30) HAS SHOWN SUBSTANTIAL GROWTH OVER THE PERIOD.  AN
ARCH FILAMENT SYSTEM CONTINUES IN PROGRESS, BUT THUS FAR THE

MODERATE.  THE INTERACTION BETWEEN THE COMPLEX OF REGIONS IN THE
NORTH CENTRAL PORTION OF THE DISK WILL BE THE MOST LIKELY
SOURCE OF THIS ACTIVITY.

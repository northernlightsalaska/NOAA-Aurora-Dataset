JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 307 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 03 NOV 1990
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 02/2100Z
TO 03/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. AN ERUPTIVE PROMINENCE
ON THE NORTHWEST LIMB PROMPTED AN X-RAY ENHANCEMENT OF C1.3 AT
02/2158Z. REGION 6350 (S25E25) PRODUCED THE ONLY FLARE OF THE
PERIOD, A C1/SF EVENT AT 03/0335Z. REGIONS 6345 (S04W04) AND
6347 (S19E24) REMAINED LARGELY STABLE WITH ONLY MINOR GROWTH
NOTED. WEAK SURGING WAS OBSERVED ON THE NORTHEAST LIMB.
NEW REGIONS 6351 (N12W11), 6352 (N08E04), AND 6353 (N12E76)
WERE ASSIGNED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW.

FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED WITH ACTIVE CONDITIONS POSSIBLE
BY THE END OF THE PERIOD.

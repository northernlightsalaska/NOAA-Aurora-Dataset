IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 13/2100Z
TO 14/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY CONTINUED MODERATE. REGION 5882
(N21E39) PRODUCED AN M2/1F TENFLARE AT 1008Z. IT HAS BEEN
MOSTLY QUIET SINCE ALTHOUGH ARCH FILAMENTS AND ACTIVE FILAMENTS
WERE SEEN ALONG THE NEUTRAL LINE. ARCH FILAMENTS WERE ALSO
OBSERVED IN REGION 5884 (N34E32) BUT ITS ACTIVITY CONSISTED OF
BUT A FEW SUBFLARES. SURGING WAS REPORTED AT SW12 NEAR THE SITE
OF REGION 5870 AND ALSO AT EAST LIMB NEAR N25 WHERE SPOTS MAY
BE COMING INTO VIEW.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO
PERSIST AT THE MODERATE LEVEL. REGION 5882 HAS PRODUCED
ONE M-CLASS FLARE PER DAY AND, BARRING UNFORESEEN CHANGE,
THIS PERFORMANCE IS LIKELY TO CONTINUE.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 13/2100Z TO 14/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET UNTIL MIDWAY THROUGH THE
PERIOD WHEN UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE CONDITIONS SET IN.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED.

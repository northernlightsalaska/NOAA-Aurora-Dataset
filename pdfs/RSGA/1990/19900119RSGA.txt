N24 AND S09.

MODERATE WITH A SLIGHT CHANCE FOR HIGH LEVELS. REGIONS 5890 AND
5882 APPEAR TO BE THE PRIMARY THREATS FOR M-CLASS OR BETTER
FLARE ACTIVITY.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 29/2100Z
TO 30/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5892 (S27W91)
PRODUCED AN M1/1F FLARE AT 29/2124Z. THE FLARE WAS ACCOMPANIED
BY A SIGNIFICANT TYPE II SWEEP WITH AN ESTIMATED VELOCITY OF
1000 KM/SEC. THEREAFTER, THE REGION DISPLAYED A QUIET LIMB
TRANSIT. REGION 5900 (S10W43) REMAINED FAIRLY QUIET DESPITE THE
FORMATION OF A DELTA MAGNETIC STRUCTURE WITHIN ITS TRAILER
SPOTS. NEW REGION 5919 (N08E48), A SMALL BIPOLE, WAS NUMBERED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE. REGION 5900 MAY PRODUCE AN ISOLATED M-FLARE.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 29/2100Z TO 30/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT MOSTLY UNSETTLED LEVELS AT
MIDDLE LATITUDES FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS. THE HIGH LATITUDE
FIELD RANGED FROM UNSETTLED TO MINOR STORM LEVELS. THE
ORIGIN OF THE STORMING IS UNCLEAR AT THIS TIME.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED.

LOW. HOWEVER, CHANCES FOR MODERATE ACTIVITY WILL INCREASE
THROUGH THE PERIOD IN ANTICIPATION OF THE RETURN OF OLD REGIONS
6223 AND 6226 ON 13-14 SEPTEMBER. BOTH REGIONS WERE MAJOR FLARE
PRODUCERS LAST ROTATION.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 11/2100Z TO 12/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE LEVELS
FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS. BRIEF PERIODS OF MINOR STORMING WERE
RECORDED AT HIGHER LATITUDES. THE ACTIVE TO MINOR STORM
LEVELS WERE PROBABLY IN RESPONSE TO CORONAL HOLE EFFECTS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY ACTIVE DECREASING TO UNSETTLED LEVELS AT
MIDDLE LATITUDES. THE HIGH LATITUDE FIELD IS EXPECTED TO BE
ACTIVE.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 29/2100Z
TO 30/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW.  REGION 6077 (N20E01)
CONTINUES TO SLOWLY DECAY.  THIS REGION HAS PRODUCED ONLY A FEW
SUBFLARES SINCE YESTERDAY.  REMAINING REGIONS WERE QUIET.
NEW REGION 6083 (N13E22) WAS NUMBERED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW.  REGION 6077 MAY PRODUCE C CLASS ACTIVITY.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 29/2100Z TO 30/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET TO UNSETTLED UNTIL ABOUT
0300Z ON 30 MAY.  AFTERWARDS, THE FIELD REACHED ACTIVE TO
STORM LEVELS.  THIS DISTURBANCE COULD BE RELATED TO THE
DISAPPEARANCE OF A RATHER LARGE, BUSHY FILAMENT ON 25-26 MAY.
THE PROTON EVENT WHICH BEGAN AT 28/0715Z ENDED AT 30/1100Z.
PEAK FLUXES REACHED WERE 45 PFU AT 29/0100Z (GT 10 MEV),
11 PFU AT 28/1520Z (GT 50 MEV), AND 4 PFU AT 28/1520Z
(GT 100 MEV).
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE.

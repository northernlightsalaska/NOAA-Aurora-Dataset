IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 07/2100Z
TO 08/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY REACHED MODERATE LEVELS TODAY
AT 08/0706Z WITH THE OCCURRENCE OF AN UNCORELATED M1 X-RAY
EVENT, THE FIRST OBSERVED SINCE 25 SEPTEMBER. OTHER
HIGHLIGHTS OF THE PERIOD INCLUDED OCCASIONAL C-CLASS
SUBFLARES FROM REGIONS 6309 (N11E59) AND 6311 (N14E67).

AND NORTHEAST QUADRANTS OF THE DISK DISAPPEARED DURING THE
PERIOD. NEW REGIONS 6312 (N10E73), 6313 (S13E75), 6314
(S18E76), AND 6315 (S06W73) WERE ASSIGNED.

FLARING.

IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 10/2100Z TO 11/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE LEVELS
FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS AT MIDDLE LATITUDES. THE HIGHER

BEGAN NEAR 11/1200Z, PROBABLY IN RESPONSE TO A LARGE,
FAVORABLY POSITIONED CORONAL HOLE.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE ACTIVE. PERIODS OF MINOR STORMING MAY OCCUR
TOMORROW, ESPECIALLY AT THE HIGHER LATITUDES, AS CORONAL HOLE
EFFECTS CONTINUE. THESE EFFECTS SHOULD BEGIN TO SUBSIDE ON THE
FINAL DAY OF THE FORECAST PERIOD.

JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 257 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 14 SEP 1990
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 13/2100Z
TO 14/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. HIGHLIGHTS OF
THE PERIOD INCLUDE AN M1/1F FLARE AT 14/0417Z FROM REGION
6266 (S28E31) WHICH WAS ACCOMPANIED BY A MAJOR TYPE II
SWEEP. A LONG DURATION M3 X-RAY EVENT WAS NOTED AT 14/1406Z
ORIGINATING FROM AN AREA JUST AROUND THE SOUTHEAST (S20)
LIMB. ONLY SUBFLARING WAS VISIBLE AT THIS LOCATION HOWEVER
A LOOP PROMINENCE SYSTEM AND EVENT LEVEL BRIGHT SURGING WAS
EVIDENT. A SIGNIFICANT TYPE II SWEEP WAS ALSO NOTED IN
CONJUNCTION WITH THIS EVENT WHICH APPEARS TO BE SIGNALING THE
RETURN OF OLD REGION 6226 (S22). AN M1/2N FLARE AT 14/2015Z
FROM REGION 6263 (N10E25) CLOSED OUT THE PERIOD. A SIGNIFICANT
TYPE II SWEEP ACCOMPANIED THIS EVENT. NO MAJOR GROWTH IN SPOT
AREA WAS NOTED TODAY. NEW REGIONS 6272 (S12E72), 6273
(N05W26), AND 6274 (S09W07) WERE ASSIGNED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. NEW REGIONS EMERGING ON THE SOUTHEAST LIMB ARE
EXPECTED TO PROVIDE AN INCREASING THREAT FOR M-CLASS FLARING.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 13/2100Z TO 14/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE
LEVELS FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE WITH ACTIVE CONDITIONS
PREDOMINENT TOWARDS THE END OF THE PERIOD.

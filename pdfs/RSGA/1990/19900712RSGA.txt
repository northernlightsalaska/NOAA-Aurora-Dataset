TO 12/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE.  AN M1.2 FLARE
OCCURRED FROM A RECENTLY EMERGING REGION, 6151 (N10W19),

REMAINING ACTIVE REGIONS.

IS EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO SLIGHTLY ACTIVE DUE TO POSSIBLE
INFLUENCE BY A FAVORABLY POSITIONED CORONAL HOLE.

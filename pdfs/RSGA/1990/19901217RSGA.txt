IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 16/2100Z
TO 17/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 6412 (N16W04)
PRODUCED AN M1/1B FLARE AT 17/0642Z AND AN M2/1B FLARE AT
17/1529Z. MINOR RADIO EMISSIONS ACCOMPANIED THE FLARES. THE
TRAILER PORTION OF THIS REGION IS MAGNETICALLY COMPLEX AND HAS
BEEN THE SITE OF MOST OF THE FLARING IN THIS REGION. REGION 6415
(N13W15) DEVELOPED RAPIDLY IN AREAL AND MAGNETIC ASPECTS AND
PRODUCED THREE SUBFLARES. MINOR DECAY WAS EVIDENT IN REGION 6410
(N03W31). NO NEW REGIONS WERE ASSIGNED.

MODERATE. REGIONS 6412 AND 6415 MAY PRODUCE M-CLASS FLARES.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 26/2100Z
TO 27/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5974 (S33W88)
GENERATED AN M1/1N AT 0419Z. SINCE THEN SURGING HAS ACCOMPANIED
ITS WEST LIMB PASSAGE. REGION 5984 (S12W57) FORMED A WEAK DELTA
IN THE NORTHEASTERN PART OF THE GROUP, BUT ITS FLARE OUTPUT
TODAY HAS BEEN MODEST. REGION 5988 (S06W26), THE OTHER
FAIRLY LARGE REGION VISIBLE, DECAYED SLIGHTLY AND SIMPLIFIED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. REGIONS 5984 AND 5988 HAVE THE BEST POTENTIAL FOR
OCCASIONAL M-CLASS ACTIVITY.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 26/2100Z TO 27/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS MOSTLY ACTIVE AT MID-LATITUDES.
ACTIVE TO MINOR STORM LEVELS PREVAILED AT HIGH LATITUDES.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE THROUGHOUT THE FORECAST
PERIOD. THE CURRENT DISTURBANCE, PERHAPS RELATED TO A CORONAL
HOLE, SHOULD GRADUALLY WEAKEN WITH TIME.

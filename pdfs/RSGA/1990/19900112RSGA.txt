IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 11/2100Z
TO 12/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5871 (S24W60)
PRODUCED AN M1/SF WITH TYPE II AT 0100Z. REGION 5882 (N21E64)
APPROACHED THAT LEVEL WITH A C8/SF AT 0759Z. THIS REGION SEEMS
TO BE THE MOST DYNAMIC SPOT GROUP NOW VISIBLE. NEWLY ASSIGNED
REGIONS ARE 5883 (S19E66) AND 5884 (N33E60).
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. OCCASIONAL M-CLASS FLARES MAY OCCUR IN REGIONS
5882, 5871, AND TO A LESSER DEGREE, 5873 (N26W81).
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 11/2100Z TO 12/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN MOSTLY QUIET TO UNSETTLED.
SUBSTORMS CONTINUED IN MIDNIGHT SECTORS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED AT MID-LATITUDES. HIGH
LATITUDES SHOULD BE GENERALLY ACTIVE.

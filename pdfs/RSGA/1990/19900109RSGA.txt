IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 08/2100Z
TO 09/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE.  REGION 5871 (S23W21)
PRODUCED TWO M FLARES, AN M2/1B AT 09/1450UT AND AN M1/1N AT
09/2039UT.  THIS REGION IS GROWING, DOUBLING IN SIZE IN THE
PAST 24 HOURS.  AFS HAS BEEN REPORTED.  FLARES ARE SMALL AND
COMPACT.  THE OTHER REGIONS OF INTEREST, REGION 5874 (S14E03)
AND REGION 5864 (N15W75), HAVE BEEN RELATIVELY QUIET.  REGION
5874 IS AN ELONGATED REGION, BUT IS REPORTED AS A BETA GAMMA.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE OVER THE PERIOD WITH REGIONS 5871 AND 5874
BEING THE MAIN CONTRIBUTORS.

NOTED AT HIGH LATITUDES.

EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO QUIET OVER THE PERIOD.  SOME SHORT
ACTIVE PERIODS ARE POSSIBLE AT HIGH LATITUDES.

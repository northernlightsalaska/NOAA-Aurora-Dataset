SDF NUMBER 200 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 19 JUL 1990
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 18/2100Z
TO 19/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW.  ONLY AN OCCASIONAL
C CLASS FLARE WAS OBSERVED SINCE YESTERDAY.  GROWTH IN REGION
6161 (S28E41) HAS SLOWED AND THE DELTA CONFIGURATION IS NO
LONGER PRESENT.  TRAILER SUNSPOTS TO REGION 6162 (N10E68) HAVE
ROTATED INTO VIEW.  THIS REGION APPEARS TO POSSESS SOME
COMPLEXITY BUT HAS NOT FLARED SINCE ITS DISK ARRIVAL.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE.  M CLASS FLARES ARE POSSIBLE IN REGIONS 6161 AND 6162.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 18/2100Z TO 19/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET TO ACTIVE.  THE FIELD WAS MOSTLY
QUIET TO UNSETTLED UNTIL ABOUT 19/0300Z, AFTERWHICH IT GRADUALLY
BECAME UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE FOR THE NEXT 24 HOURS
AND QUIET TO UNSETTLED THEREAFTER.

LOW TO MODERATE TOMORROW AND MOSTLY LOW FOR THE REMAINDER OF

THE DISK.

THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD RANGED FROM QUIET TO ACTIVE LEVELS DURING
THE DAY. QUIET TO UNSETTLED LEVELS DOMINATED MOST OF THE PERIOD
WITH THE EXCEPTION OF 0000-0900UT WHEN MID-LATITUDES WERE
GENERALLY ACTIVE AND HIGH LATITUDES WERE AT ACTIVE TO MINOR
STORM LEVELS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY UNSETTLED WITH OCCASIONAL PERIODS OF
ACTIVE CONDITIONS DURING THE NEXT THREE DAYS.

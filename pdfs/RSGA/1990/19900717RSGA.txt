JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 198 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 17 JUL 1990
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 16/2100Z
TO 17/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW.  REGION 6161 (S28E64) IS
THE LARGEST AND MOST ACTIVE SUNSPOT GROUP ON THE DISK.  THIS
REGION APPEARS TO BE DEVELOPING IN SIZE AND COMPLEXITY.
REGIONS 6151 (S09W80) AND 6153 (N15W87) ARE DECAYING AS THEY
APPROACH THE WEST LIMB.  REMAINING REGIONS WERE QUIET.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE.  REGION 6161 MAY PRODUCE M CLASS ACTIVITY.

THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET TO UNSETTLED.

EXPECTED TO BE ACIVE ON 18 JULY IN RESPONSE TO THE DSF/LDE
ON 15 JULY.  THE REMAINDER OF THE PERIOD SHOULD BE QUIET TO
UNSETTLED.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 24/2100Z
TO 25/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW.  SEVERAL SMALL C-CLASS
FLARES WERE OBSERVED SINCE YESTERDAY, MOST OF WHICH ORIGINATED
IN REGIONS 6327 (N20W34) AND 6337 (S14E29).  A NUMBER OF NEW
SPOTS EMERGED IN THE CENTRAL PORTION OF REGION 6327.  THIS NEW

TRAILER POLARITY PRECEDING THOSE OF LEADER POLARITY.  GROWTH IN
REGION 6337 HAS SUBSIDED.  NEW REGIONS 6340 (S28W56) AND
6341 (N06E39) WERE NUMBERED.

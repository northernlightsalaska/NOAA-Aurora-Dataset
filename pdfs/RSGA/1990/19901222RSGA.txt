IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 21/2100Z
TO 22/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 6420
(S22E55) PRODUCED THE ONLY M-FLARE OF THE PERIOD: AN M1/SF
EVENT AT 22/1548Z. SEVERAL C-CLASS SUBFLARES WERE ALSO
ATTRIBUTED TO THIS GROUP WHICH SHOWS SOME MAGNETIC COMPLEXITY
IN ITS TRAILING PORTION. OTHER ACTIVITY OF NOTE INCLUDED A
C9/1N FLARE AT 21/2233Z FROM REGION 6412 (N17W68). REGION
6415 (N13W81) REMAINED GENERALLY STABLE WITH OCCASIONAL WEAK
PLAGE FLUCTUATIONS OBSERVED. NEW REGION 6422 (S18E68) WAS
ASSIGNED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. REGIONS 6415 AND 6412 MAY PRODUCE ISOLATED M-CLASS
FLARES PRIOR TO THEIR DEPARTURES ON 23-24 DECEMBER. REGION
6420 MAY ALSO PRODUCE M-CLASS FLARING.

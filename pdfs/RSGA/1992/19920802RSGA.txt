SDF NUMBER 215 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 02 AUG 1992
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 01/2100Z
TO 02/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. REGION 7248 (S12E66) PRO-
DUCED MOST OF TODAY'S FREQUENT C-CLASS FLARE ACTIVITY INCLUDING
THE LARGEST EVENT: A C7/SN AT 1939Z. THE REGION ALSO PRODUCED A
C1/SN AT 1431Z WHICH LED TO A BRIGHT SURGE BEING VISIBLE BEYOND
THE SOLAR LIMB. REGION 7241 (N13W22) SHOWED NEW GROWTH AND
DEVELOPMENT TODAY IN A REVERSE POLARITY CONFIGURATION: THE GROUP
PRODUCED A FEW SUBFLARES DURING THE PERIOD. REGION 7242 (N18W07)
ALSO SHOWED NEW GROWTH AND PRODUCED SOME SMALL FLARES.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MOSTLY LOW. REGION 7248 IS EXPECTED TO DOMINATE ACTIVITY WITH
OCCASIONAL CONTRIBUTIONS FROM 7241 AND 7242. THERE IS A SLIGHT
CHANCE FOR AN ISOLATED M-CLASS EVENT OVER THE NEXT THREE DAYS.

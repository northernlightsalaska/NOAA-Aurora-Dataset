MAJOR STORM LEVELS. THIS ACTIVITY WAS PROBABLY IN RESPONSE
TO CORONAL HOLE EFFECTS.

EXPECTED TO BE ACTIVE ON THE FIRST DAY DECLINING TO UNSETTLED
LEVELS DURING THE REMAINDER OF THE PERIOD AS CORONAL HOLE

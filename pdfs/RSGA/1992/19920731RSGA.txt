SDF NUMBER 213 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 31 JUL 1992
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 30/2100Z
TO 31/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. REGION 7242 (N18E19) PRO-
DUCED A FEW C-CLASS FLARES DURING THE PERIOD: THE LARGEST WAS A
C3/SF AT 2236Z. MOST OF THE ACTIVITY IS JUST NORTHEAST OF THE
LEADER SPOT WHERE A SMALL ISLAND OF TRAILER POLARITY MAGNETIC
FLUX APPEARS TO BE EMERGING. SEVERAL NEW GROUPS WERE ASSIGNED
TODAY AS SMALL BXO TYPE REGIONS: 7245 (S06E64), 7246 (S05W13),
AND 7247 (N06W45). SURGING WAS OBSERVED ON EAST LIMB AT S11.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW. A GRADUAL INCREASE IN ACTIVITY AND BACKGROUND LEVELS IS
ANTICIPATED BEGINNING TOMORROW WITH THE RETURN OF OLD REGIONS
7220 (S13, L-030) AND 7222 (S11, L-013).
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 30/2100Z TO 31/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS MOSTLY QUIET TO UNSETTLED. A BRIEF
ACTIVE PERIOD WAS OBSERVED AT SOME SITES FROM 0900-1200UT.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED.

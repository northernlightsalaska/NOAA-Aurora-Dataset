SDF NUMBER 243 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 30 AUG 1992
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 29/2100Z
TO 30/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. REGION 7270 (S09E52)
PRODUCED ALL OF THE OBSERVED FLARES DURING THE PAST DAY. THE
LARGEST WAS A C2/SF AT 30/0708Z. THIS REGION REMAINS A
RELATIVELY SIMPLE SUNSPOT GROUP AND APPEARS TO BE ALL THAT IS
LEFT OF OLD ACTIVE REGION 7248. OTHER REGIONS WERE QUIET.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW. SMALL FLARES ARE EXPECTED IN REGION 7270.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 29/2100Z TO 30/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS MOSTLY QUIET. SUBSTORM ACTIVITY WAS
OBSERVED AT SOME STATIONS FROM 30/0900-1500Z.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED.

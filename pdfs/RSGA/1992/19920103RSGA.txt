SDF NUMBER 003 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 03 JAN 1992
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 02/2100Z
TO 03/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 6993 (S09E30)
PRODUCED THREE M1 X-RAY FLARES. ONE OF WHICH WAS A LONG DURATION
EVENT PEAKING AT 1716Z. AN M3/1N FLARE ERUPTED IN REGION 6994
(S16E36) AT 0943Z. THIS AREA REMAINED HOT DURING MOST OF THE
PERIOD WITH SOME GROWTH NOTED. REGION 6988 (S14W59) REMAINED
GENERALLY QUIET. REGION 6985 (S18W78) ALSO SHOWED LITTLE ACTION.
AN UNCORRELATED C7 X-RAY EVENT AT 0717Z WAS ACCOMPANIED BY A
WEAK TYPE II RADIO SWEEP. NEW REGIONS 6997 (S21E37) AND 6998
(N03E69) WERE ASSIGNED. 6997 IS STACKED SOUTH OF 6994 AND MAY
PROVIDE ADDITIONAL INTERACTION IN THIS ALREADY COMPLEX AREA.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. ISOLATED M-CLASS FLARING REMAINS POSSIBLE FROM
REGIONS 6985, 6988, 6993, AND 6994.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 02/2100Z TO 03/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN QUIET TO UNSETTLED.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO SLIGHTLY ACTIVE ON THE 4TH DUE TO
EFFECTS FROM A CORONAL HOLE AND FROM FLARING NOTED LATE ON THE

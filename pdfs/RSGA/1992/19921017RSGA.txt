IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 16/2100Z

STILL A RELATIVELY SMALL SUNSPOT GROUP. NEW REGIONS INCLUDE

7293 (S07, L-069) RETURNS AT THE EAST LIMB.

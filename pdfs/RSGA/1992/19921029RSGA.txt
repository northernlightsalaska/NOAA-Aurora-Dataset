IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 28/2100Z
TO 29/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 7321 (S25W55)

UNCORRELATED M1, OCCURRED AT 29/0740UT.

ACTIVITY. THIS REGION STILL HAS SMALL POTENTIAL FOR AN ISOLATED
X-CLASS AND PROTON FLARE.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 28/2100Z TO 29/2100Z:

FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS. A SHORT PERIOD OF MINOR STORM LEVELS
AT LOW LATITUDE STATIONS WAS SEEN AT 29/0900UT. HIGH LATITUDE
STATIONS EXPERIENCED PERIODS OF MAJOR STORM LEVELS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO REMAIN ACTIVE FOR THE NEXT 24 HOURS, THEN SETTLE
DOWN TO MOSTLY UNSETTLED LEVELS FOR THE REMAINDER OF THE PERIOD.

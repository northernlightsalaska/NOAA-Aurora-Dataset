IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 01/2100Z

SIGNIFICANT GROWTH WAS 7152 (N13E27). IT PRODUCED A SINGLE
SUBFLARE EARLY IN THE PERIOD. NEW REGION 7154 (S25E76) ROTATED
INTO VIEW TODAY.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 01/2100Z TO 02/2100Z:

FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS. THE MINOR STORMING WAS BRIEF AND
CENTERED NEAR 02/0600Z. IT WAS PROBABLY DUE TO CORONAL HOLE
EFFECTS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE DURING THE FIRST DAY DUE TO
CONTINUING CORONAL HOLE EFFECTS. THE FIELD IS EXPECTED TO
SUBSIDE TO UNSETTLED TO QUIET LEVELS FOR THE REMAINDER OF THE
PERIOD.

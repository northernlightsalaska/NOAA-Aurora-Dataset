SDF NUMBER 189 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 07 JUL 1992
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 06/2100Z
TO 07/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 7220 (S11E50)
PRODUCED AN M4/SB FLARE AT 07/1140Z. THIS REGION ALSO PRODUCED
SEVERAL C-CLASS SUFLARES SINCE YESTERDAY. THE REGION CONTINUES
TO GROW IN WHITE LIGHT BUT DOES NOT APPEAR TO BE PARTICULARLY
COMPLEX MAGNETICALLY. REGION 7216 (N14W35) CONTINUES TO DECAY
SLOWLY AND HAS PRODUCED ONLY A FEW SUBFLARES. NEW REGIONS
7222 (S11E63) AND 7223 (S14W09) WERE NUMBERED. REGION 7222 IS
DEVELOPING NEAR THE TRAILER PORTION OF 7220 AND MAY HAVE TO BE
COMBINED WITH 7220 IF FUTURE GROWTH MAKES SEPARATION DIFFICULT.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. ADDITIONAL M-CLASS ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED IN REGION
7220. REGION 7216 MAY PRODUCE AN M-CLASS FLARE YET.

JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 027 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 27 JAN 1992
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 26/2100Z
TO 27/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. ONLY ISOLATED C-CLASS
FLARING WAS NOTED. MOST OF THESE EVENTS WERE ATTRIBUTED TO
REGION 7031 (S04E32) WHICH CONTINUES TO GROW. GROWTH WAS ALSO
NOTED IN REGION 7026 (S12W13), 7030 (N07E34), AND 7032 (N07E22).
SEVERAL UNCORRELATED L0W-LEVEL C-CLASS EVENTS WERE OBSERVED.
THESE MAY HAVE BEEN ASSOCIATED WITH THE NEW REGIONS EMERGING ON
THE EASTERN LIMB. NEW REGIONS ASSIGNED INCLUDE 7034 (N08E67),
7035 (S21E74), 7036 (S14E69), 7037 (N06E29) AND 7038 (S12E20).
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE. GENERALLY C-CLASS FLARING IS EXPECTED. REGION
7031 MAY PRODUCE AN ISOLATED M-FLARE.

OBSERVED AT 26/2241Z.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED.

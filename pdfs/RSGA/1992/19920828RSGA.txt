JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 241 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 28 AUG 1992
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 27/2100Z
TO 28/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS VERY LOW. ONLY A FEW B-CLASS
FLARES HAVE BEEN OBSERVED SINCE YESTERDAY, ALL OF WHICH OCCURRED
IN REGION 7268 (N05W63). THIS REGION HAS COMPACT, BRIGHT PLAGE
AND IS GROWING SLOWLY IN WHITE LIGHT. REGION 7267 (N02W02)
HAS ALSO GROWN BUT HAS NOT PRODUCED ANY FLARES. SURGING IS
IN PROGRESS AT THE EAST LIMB NEAR S10, THE RETURNING LOCATION
OF OLD REGION 7248.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
VERY LOW TO LOW. C-CLASS FLARES ARE POSSIBLE IN REGION 7268.
ACTIVITY FROM THE SOUTHEAST LIMB IS ALSO POSSIBLE.

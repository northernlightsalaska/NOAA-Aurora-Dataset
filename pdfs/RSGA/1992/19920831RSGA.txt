JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 244 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 31 AUG 1992
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 30/2100Z
TO 31/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS VERY LOW. REGION 7270 (S09E40)
PRODUCED SEVERAL SUBFLARES BUT ALL OF THEM HAD LESS THAN C
CLASS OUTPUT IN X-RAYS. THIS REGION REMAINS A SMALL,
MAGNETICALLY SIMPLE, SUNSPOT GROUP. NEW REGION 7271 (S09E27)
WAS NUMBERED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
VERY LOW TO LOW. SMALL FLARES ARE EXPECTED IN REGION 7270.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 30/2100Z TO 31/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS MOSTLY QUIET.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED FOR THE FIRST TWO DAYS OF
THE FORECAST PERIOD. ACTIVE TO STORM CONDITIONS ARE POSSIBLE
ON THE THIRD DAY DUE TO A CORONAL HOLE.

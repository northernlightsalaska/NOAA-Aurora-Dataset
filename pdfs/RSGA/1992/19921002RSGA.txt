IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 01/2100Z
TO 02/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE BY VIRTUE OF A SINGLE
M1/1N FLARE AT 02/0751UT IN NEW REGION 7301 (S16E38). THIS
REGION ALSO PRODUCED THREE SMALL C CLASS FLARES TODAY. THE ONLY
OTHER FLARE PRODUCER WAS REGION 7293 (S07W58) WITH A SINGLE
C2/SF AT 01/2312UT. THREE REGIONS WERE NUMBERED; 7301, 7302
(S12E08) AND 7303 (S12E47).

LOW TO MODERATE. REGION 7301 IS EXPECTED TO CONTINUE PRODUCING
C CLASS FLARES AND HAS THE BEST CHANCE OF PRODUCING AN M CLASS
FLARE.

IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY UNSETTLED.

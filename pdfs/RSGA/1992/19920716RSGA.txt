IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 15/2100Z

TENFLARE AT 1659Z. THE TENFLARE WAS 710 SFU. THIS FLARE
PRODUCED A TYPE II SWEEP. THIS COMPLEX POSSESSES THE HIGHEST

PERIOD. THERE IS A SLIGHT CHANCE FOR AN X-CLASS EVENT AS WELL.

THE M6 FLARE, DISAPPEARING FILAMENTS, AND CORONAL HOLES

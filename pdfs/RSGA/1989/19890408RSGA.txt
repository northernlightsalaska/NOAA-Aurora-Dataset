IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 07/2100Z
TO 08/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. REGION COMPLEX 5441
(N35E21) IS COMPOSED OF THREE ABUTTED/STACKED SPOT GROUPS. IT
PRODUCED SMALL C-CLASS SUBFLARES, BUT WITH NO SIGNIFICANT RADIO
PARAMETERS. THE OVERALL X-RAY BACKGROUND THRESHOLD LEVELS HAVE
STEADILY INCREASED DURING THE PERIOD.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE. THE SLOW X-RAY FLUX INCREASE IMPLIES AN ACTIVE
SECTOR APPROACHING THE EAST LIMB. FLARE PROBABILITIES INCREASE
SLOWLY THROUGH THE PERIOD.

THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS AT ACTIVE TO MINOR STORM LEVELS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE ACTIVE.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 01/2100Z
TO 02/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS HIGH. REGION 5517 (S18E53)
PRODUCED AN X1/2B TENFLARE AT 1605Z FOLLOWED BY A TYPE II
RADIO SWEEP. THIS REGION ALSO GENERATED THREE M-CLASS EVENTS;
M2/0F AT 0457Z, M1/0F AT 1800Z AND AN M1/1B AT 1816Z. REGION
5517 SHOWS SPOT AND MAGNETIC COMPLEXITY NOW EVIDENT AS IT
ROTATES FURTHER INTO VIEW. A DELTA CONFIGURATION IS APPARENT
IN THE INTERMEDIATE PENUMBRA. REGION 5511 (S21W31) YIELDED AN
M1/0F AT 1738Z AND REMAINS COMPLEX IN ITS SPOT AND MAGNETIC
STRUCTURE. SURGING AND BRIGHT PLAGE HAVE BEEN OBSERVED AT
S27E88.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE TO HIGH. REGIONS 5517 AND 5511 SHOULD BE THE PRIMARY
CONTRIBUTORS TO THE ACTIVITY.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 01/2100Z TO 02/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 11/2100Z
TO 12/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. SEVERAL SUBFLARES OCCURRED
DURING THE PERIOD, NONE OF WHICH WERE ASSOCIATED WITH
SIGNIFICANT RADIO OR X-RAY EMISSIONS. AN ERUPTIVE PROMINENCE
ON LIMB (EPL) OCCURRED AT 12/1015Z NEAR S12W76. IT EXTENDED TO
0.13 SOLAR RADII. TWO FILAMENTS DISAPPEARED LATE IN THE PERIOD.
THE FIRST, LOCATED AT N42W19, WAS 28 DEGREES IN EXTENT AND
DISAPPEARED AT 12/1800Z. THE SECOND DISAPPEARED AT 12/1930Z
FROM N37W21 AND WAS 9 DEGREES IN EXTENT. NEW REGION 5591
(N13W35) EMERGED AS A SMALL UNIPOLE.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW.

24 HOURS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY UNSETTLED.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 11/2100Z
TO 12/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. NEW REGION 5836 (N13E77)
PRODUCED A C6/LOOP PROMINENCE SYSTEM AT 12/1906Z. THIS REGION
APPEARED AS A MODERATE SIZE H SPOT WITH SMALL TRAILER SPOTS.
OTHERWISE, ONLY INFREQUENT SMALL CLASS C FLARES OCCURRED.
A SMALL REGION ROTATED AROUND THE LIMB AND WAS NUMBERED
AS REGION 5835 (N27E74). OTHER REGIONS GENERALLY DECLINED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY SHOULD CONTINUE
AT A LOW LEVEL FOR THE FORECAST PERIOD. ISOLATED CLASS M
FLARES ARE POSSIBLE FROM REGIONS 5836 AND 5821 (N19W57).
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 11/2100Z TO 12/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET DURING THE PERIOD.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD
SHOULD BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED ON 13 DEC. ON 14-15 DEC, LOW
LATITUDES SHOULD EXPERIENCE GENERALLY UNSETTLED CONDITIONS.
HIGH LATITUDES DURING THAT TIME SHOULD EXPERIENCE ACTIVE TO
MINOR STORM CONDITIONS. THIS DISTURBANCE IS FORECAST BASED
UPON A MODERATE DURATION C9 FLARE OBSERVED AT 11/1849Z.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 21/2100Z
TO 22/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW.  REGION 5698 (S25W11)
PRODUCED SEVERAL C-CLASS FLARES SINCE YESTERDAY.  THIS REGION
HAS STARTED TO SHOW SIGNS OF DECAY, HOWEVER THE SUNSPOTS
REMAIN LARGE AND COMPLEX.  REGIONS 5705 (N21W55) AND 5706
(S18E46) WERE NUMBERED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE TO HIGH.  REGION 5698 HAS THE POTENTIAL TO PRODUCE
A MAJOR FLARE.  OLD REGION 5669 (S17 L-085, CLASS/AREA FKC/
3080 ON 03 SEPTEMBER) SHOULD BEGIN TO APPEAR ON 25 SEPTEMBER.

MIDDLE LATITUDE STATIONS AT 22/0741Z.  THE SOURCE OF THIS
DISTURBANCE IS UNKNOWN AT THIS TIME.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE.

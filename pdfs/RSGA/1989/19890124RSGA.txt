IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 23/2100Z
TO 24/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGIONS 5329 (N18E22)
AND 5330 (S17E52) WERE COMPLICATED MAGNETICALLY. REGION 5329 HAS
CONSISTENTLY PRODUCED VERY LOW M-CLASS FLARES, AND CONTINUES TO
BE THE DOMINANT COMPLEX ON THE DISK.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODEREATE. REGION 5329 PROVIDES A VERY SLIGHT CHANCE OF BOTH
HIGH AND PROTON PRODUCING FLARE ACTIVITY.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 23/2100Z TO 24/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS UNSETTLED.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE ACTIVE TO UNSETTLED. A DISTURBANCE IS EXPECTED
IN RESPONSE TO A SOUTHERN CORONAL HOLE. BRIEF STORM CONDITIONS
ARE PROBABLE AT HIGHER LATITUDES.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 04/2100Z
TO 05/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. REGION 5354 (N32E65)

SOUTHWEST LIMB EARLY IN THE PERIOD. NO NEW REGIONS WERE ASSIGNED
TODAY.

MODERATE. REGION 5354 PROVIDES A GOOD CHANCE FOR AN M-CLASS
FLARE. THERE IS ALSO A SLIGHT CHANCE FOR A MAJOR FLARE FROM
THIS REGION.

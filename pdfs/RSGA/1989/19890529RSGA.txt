IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 28/2100Z
TO 29/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5497 (S20W86)
PRODUCED AN M2/1N AT 29/0334Z AND A LONG DURATION M3/1F AT
29/0433Z WITH A WEAK TYPE II AND A LOOP PROMINENCE SYSTEM TO
.14R ABOVE THE WEST LIMB. THIS REGION PRODUCED ANOTHER TYPE II
WITH A C8/SF FLARE AT 28/2303Z. REGION 5506 (N21E04) PRODUCED
AN M1/1N AT 28/2215Z WITH MINOR RADIO BURSTS. A FILAMENT ABOUT
10 DEGREES EAST OF REGION 5506 FADED EITHER WITH OR SHORTLY
AFTER THIS FLARE. THIS REGION ALSO PRODUCED AN M1/1N AT
29/0254Z. GROWTH IS NOT OCCURRING IN REGION 5506.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY SHOULD CONTINUE
MODERATE FOR 30 MAY WHILE CORONAL EMISSIONS CAN STILL BE
OBSERVED FROM REGION 5497. ISOLATED CLASS M FLARES FROM
REGION 5506 ARE POSSIBLE 31 MAY - 01 JUN.

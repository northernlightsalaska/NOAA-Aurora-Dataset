ANOTHER ACTIVE PERIOD AT HIGH LATITUDES FROM 18-21Z.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE AT MOSTLY UNSETTLED LEVELS THROUGH THE FORECAST
PERIOD. ACTIVE CONDITIONS ARE EXPECTED AT HIGH LATITUDES ON

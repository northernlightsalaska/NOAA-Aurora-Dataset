IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 24/2100Z
TO 25/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. AN M1 UNCORRELATED
EVENT OCCURED AT 0246Z. REMAINING ACTIVITY WAS LIMITED
TO C-CLASS/SUBFLARES. REGION 5800 (N25E04) CONTINUES TO SLOWLY
GROW IN WHITE LIGHT STRUCTURE AND MAGNETIC COMPLEXITY. REGION
5799 (N21W42) HAS SHOWN GROWTH OF PENUMBRAL COVERAGE IN ITS
LEADER. REGIONS 5807 (N29E20) AND 5808 (S04W06) WERE ASSIGNED.
REGION 5808 IS GROWING FAST IN WHITE LIGHT WITH A STRONG ARCH
FILAMENT SYSTEM NOTED AT ITS CENTER.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE WITH C-CLASS AND OCCASIONAL M-CLASS EVENTS POSSIBLE
MAINLY FROM REGIONS 5799 OR 5800. REGIONS 5808, 5796 (N11W42),
AND 5803 (S11W07) ALSO SHOW WEAKER POTENTIAL FOR SUBFLARE
ACTIVITY.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 24/2100Z TO 25/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT QUIET LEVELS FOR
THE PAST 24 HOURS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE AT UNSETTLED LEVELS FOR THE NEXT THREE DAYS.

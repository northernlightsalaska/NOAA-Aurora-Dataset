IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 12/2100Z
TO 13/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW.  NEW REGION 5451 (N09E64),
WITH BRIGHT PLAGE INTENSITY, PRODUCED SEVERAL SUB-FAINT FLARES.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE AS REGION 5451 BEGINS ITS DISK TRANSIT.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 12/2100Z TO 13/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS AT QUIET TO ACTIVE LEVELS
WITH PERIODS OF MINOR STORM AT HIGH LATITUDES.  THE
SATELLITE PROTON EVENT AS WELL AS THE POLAR CAP ABSORPTION
EVENT CONTINUE.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY UNSETTLED WITH PERIODS OF ACTIVE
CONDITIONS.

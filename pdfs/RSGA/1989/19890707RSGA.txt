IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 06/2100Z
TO 07/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW.  REGIONS 5572 (S18W21)
AND 5575 (N24W01) EACH PRODUCED A FEW SUBFLARES AND ASSOCIATED
C-CLASS X-RAYS.  ALL DISK REGIONS ARE FAIRLY SMALL AND HAVE A
RELATIVELY SIMPLE MAGNETIC CONFIGURATION.  NEW REGION 5587
(S18W55) WAS NUMBERED TODAY.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE.  ADDITIONAL C-CLASS ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED.
THERE IS A SLIGHT CHANCE OF AN ISOLATED M-CLASS FLARE.

THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET TO UNSETTLED.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED FOR THE NEXT THREE DAYS.

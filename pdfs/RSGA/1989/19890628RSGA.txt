IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 27/2100Z
TO 28/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5569 (N17W00)
PRODUCED AN M2/2B FLARE AT 1822Z. ASSOCIATED WITH THIS EVENT
WAS A 370 SFU TENFLARE STARTING AT 1812Z (16 MINUTE DURATION).
REGION 5558 (N08W16) GENERATED A C8/SN FLARE AT 1305Z WHICH
WAS FOLLOWED BY A TYPE II BURST. REGION 5563 (S13W06) THOUGH
RELATIVELY QUIET THIS PERIOD SHOWS SPOT COMPLEXITY THAT MAY
HERALD FUTURE ENERGETIC ACTIVITY.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
AT MODERATE LEVELS. REGIONS 5563 AND 5569 SHOULD BE THE MAIN
CONTRIBUTORS TO THE FORECASTED M-CLASS ACTIVITY.

LEVELS FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE AT QUIET TO UNSETTLED AT MID LATITUDES AND
UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE LEVELS AT HIGH LATITUDES.

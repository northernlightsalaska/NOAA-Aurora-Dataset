IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 08/2100Z
TO 09/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGIONS 5464 (S19)
BEYOND WEST LIMB AND 5474 (N16W04) COMBINED TO PRODUCE THE
ONLY M-CLASS FLARE DURING THE PERIOD, AN M1 AT 1728Z. POST-FLARE
LOOPS WERE REPORTED NEAR THE SITE OF REGION 5464 CONCURRENT WITH
A SUBFLARE IN 5474. THE REMAINING EIGHT SPOTTED REGIONS WERE
MOSTLY QUIET AND STABLE. TWO NEW REGIONS WERE ASSIGNED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. LOOK FOR ISOLATED M-CLASS ACTIVITY DURING THE NEXT
THREE DAYS.

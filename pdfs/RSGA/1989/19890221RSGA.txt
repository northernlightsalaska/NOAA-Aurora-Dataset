IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 20/2100Z
TO 21/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5368 (S13W24)
GENERATED TWO M-CLASS FLARES, AN M2/0B AT 1223Z AND AN M3/1B AT
1822Z. NEITHER FLARE WAS IMPRESSIVE IN THE RADIO SPECTRUM. THE
REGION HAS GROWN IN BOTH WHITE LIGHT AND H-ALPHA AND
CURRENTLY EXHIBITS A BETA-GAMMA DELTA MAGNETIC STRUCTURE. IT
PRODUCED NEARLY ALL OF THE ACTIVITY OBSERVED DURING THE PERIOD.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY SHOULD REMAIN AT
THE MODERATE LEVEL. REGION 5368 IS LIKELY TO PRODUCE
ADDITIONAL M-CLASS ACTIVITY.

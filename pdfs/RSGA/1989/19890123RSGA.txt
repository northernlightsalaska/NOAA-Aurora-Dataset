IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 22/2100Z
TO 23/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5329 (N20E34)
IS A COMPLEX OF THREE GROUPS STACKED VERTICALLY. IT IS SLIGHTLY
COMPLICATED MAGNETICALLY IN A BETA-GAMMA CONFIGURATION, AND HAS
PRODUCED A FEW VERY SMALL M-CLASS FLARES WITH NO MAJOR RADIO
ASPECTS. REGION 5330 (S15E61) MAY HAVE A MORE MAGNETICALLY COM-
PLICATED STRUCTURE THAN CURRENT FORESHORTENED CONDITIONS CAN
SUBSTANTIATE.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW. REGION 5329 PROVIDES A VERY SLIGHT CHANCE OF BOTH HIGH AND
PROTON PRODUCING FLARE ACTIVITY.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 22/2100Z TO 23/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS ACTIVE.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE ACTIVE. A DISTURBANCE IS EXPECTED IN RESPONSE
TO A SOUTHERN CORONAL HOLE. BRIEF STORM CONDITIONS ARE
PROBABLE AT HIGHER LATITUDES.

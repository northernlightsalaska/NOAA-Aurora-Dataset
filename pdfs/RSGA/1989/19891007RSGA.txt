IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 06/2100Z
TO 07/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. NEARLY ALL OF TODAY'S
FLARE ACTIVITY WAS CONFINED TO REGION 5721 (N27E14). ITS
LARGEST EVENT WAS A C5/0F AT 0458Z. STRUCTURALLY THE REGION
HAS A SLIGHT DEGREE OF MAGNETIC COMPLEXITY. AN EPL THAT
REGISTERED C6 IN XRAYS OCCURRED AT 1323Z FROM N15E90. ONE
NEW REGION WAS ASSIGNED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE. AN ISOLATED M-CLASS FLARE MAY OCCUR IN
REGION 5721 AT SOME POINT IN THE NEXT THREE DAYS.

AT 06/1750Z.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED THROUGHOUT THE PERIOD.

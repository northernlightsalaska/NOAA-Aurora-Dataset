IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 04/2100Z
TO 05/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5622 (S27W11)
PRODUCED AN M1/1N FLARE AT 05/1028Z. AN OPTICALLY UNCORRELATED
M1 X-RAY BURST OCCURRED AT 05/0602Z. REGION 5629 (S18E46)
REMAINED IN A GROWTH PHASE THROUGHOUT THE PERIOD. IT DEVELOPED
A MAGNETIC DELTA CONFIGURATION WITHIN ITS LARGE ASSYMETRIC
LEADER SPOT, BUT WAS RELATIVELY QUIET DURING THE PERIOD. TWO
NEW REGIONS WERE ASSIGNED TODAY: 5633 (S33E46) AND 5634
(S15E79). REGION 5634 DISPLAYED ACTIVE SURGING AND PRODUCED A
C6/2N FLARE AT 05/1626Z AS IT ROTATED INTO VIEW.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. REGIONS 5629 AND 5634 ARE THE PRIMARY THREATS FOR
PRODUCING M-FLARES. REGION 5629 IS ALSO CAPABLE OF PRODUCING AN
ISOLATED MAJOR FLARE DURING THE PERIOD.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 04/2100Z TO 05/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT QUIET LEVELS FOR THE PAST
24 HOURS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE AT QUIET TO UNSETTLED LEVELS.

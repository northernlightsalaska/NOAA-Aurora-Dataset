IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 25/2100Z TO 26/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN MOSTLY QUIET AT MID-LATITUDES.
GENERALLY UNSETTLED CONDITIONS PREVAILED AT HIGH LATITUDES.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE AT ACTIVE TO MINOR STORM LEVELS THE NEXT 24
HOURS DUE TO A WELL-LOCATED CORONAL HOLE. ACTIVITY THE LAST
TWO DAYS MAY RANGE FROM UNSETTLED TO MAJOR STORM CONDITIONS AS
THE POTENTIAL GEOEFFECTIVENESS OF THE M2/2B FLARE OF 25 SEPT.
IS DIFFICULT TO ASCERTAIN.

SIGHTING. BRIEF STORM CONDITIONS ARE EXPECTED AT HIGH LATITUDES.
THE BULK OF THE EJECTION WILL PROBABLY NOT HIT US, AND ONLY AN
ENHANCEMENT OF THE FIELD IS EXPECTED.

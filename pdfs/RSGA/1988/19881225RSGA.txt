IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 24/2100Z
TO 25/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5278 (N27W88)
PRODUCED AN M1/SF FLARE AT 25/1115Z. REGION 5290 (N21E22)
EXHIBITED SLOW GROWTH. OTHER PRE-EXISTING DISK REGIONS WERE
STABLE OR DECLINING. THREE NEW SMALL REGIONS EMERGED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY SHOULD CONTINUE
MODERATE FOR 26 DEC AND DECREASE TO GENERALLY LOW LEVELS FOR
THE REMAINDER OF THE FORECAST PERIOD.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 24/2100Z TO 25/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET UNTIL APPROXIMATELY 25/0900Z
WHEN MINOR STORMING BEGAN. THE DISTURBANCE BEGAN GRADUALLY
AND IS ATTRIBUTED TO MASS EJECTIONS FROM THE WEST LIMB ON
22-23 DEC.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD
SHOULD GENERALLY BE ACTIVE ON 26 DEC WITH PERIODS AT MINOR
STORM LEVELS. UNSETTLED GEOMAGNETIC CONDITIONS SHOULD PREVAIL
FOR 27 AND 28 DEC.

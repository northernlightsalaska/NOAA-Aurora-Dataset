IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 19/2100Z
TO 20/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY CONTINUES MODERATE. REGION 5200
(N20E41) GENERATED AN M1/0N FLARE AT 19/2213Z. IN ADDITION, A
C9 X-RAY FLARE AT 0635Z IS THOUGHT TO BE ATTRIBUTABLE TO THIS
SAME REGION. BAD WEATHER HAMPERED OPTICAL PATROL DURING MUCH
OF THE PERIOD. TWO NEW REGIONS - 5203 (S23W53) AND 5204
(N21W30) - WERE NUMBERED. SURGING OCCURRED AT THE SOUTHWEST LIMB
MUCH OF THE DAY.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY SHOULD REMAIN
MODERATE. REGION 5200 IS A GOOD CANDIDATE FOR PERIODIC
M-CLASS FLARES.

STORM LEVELS WERE OBSERVED AT HIGH LATITUDE SITES.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 04/2100Z
TO 05/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. REGION 5060 (S19W54)
CONTINUED TO SLOWLY DECAY. IT RETAINED ITS BETA-GAMMA-DELTA
CONFIGURATION, BUT PRODUCED ONLY C-CLASS SUBFLARES WITHOUT
SIGNIFICANT RADIO ASPECTS. REGION 5062 (N13W49) ALSO CONTINUED
TO SLOWLY DECAY. IT RETAINED ITS BETA-GAMMA CONFIGURATION AND
ALSO PRODUCED ONLY INSIGNIFICANT SUBFLARES. NEWLY ASSIGNED
REGION 5069 (N23E53) WAS A SMALL BETA-TYPE; WHILE NEW REGION
5070 (S14E74) WAS INITIALLY SEEN AS AN ALPHA-TYPE.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW. REGION 5060 PROVIDES A VERY SLIGHT TO SLIGHT CHANCE OF BOTH
HIGH AND VERY HIGH ACTIVITY LEVELS.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 04/2100Z TO 05/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS UNSETTLED TO QUIET.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE GENERALLY UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE.

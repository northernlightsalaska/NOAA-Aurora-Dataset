IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 25/2100Z
TO 26/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5126 (S23E53)
PRODUCED AN M1/0F EVENT AT 26/1124Z WITH A WEAK TYPE II RADIO
SWEEP. THIS REGION HAS NOT CHANGED SIGNIFICANTLY THIS LAST DAY.
NEWLY ASSIGNED REGION 5128 (N21E59) WAS AN HSX TYPE. THE OTHER
NEWLY ASSIGNED REGION 5129 (S14E74) ROTATED INTO VIEW AS A DAO
TYPE. REGION 5125 (N29E55) PRODUCED NO SIGNIFICANT EVENTS BUT
APPARENTLY IS REFORMING FILAMENTS.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW. REGION 5126 PROVIDES A SLIGHT CHANCE OF MODERATE ACTIVITY,
AND A VERY SLIGHT CHANCE OF HIGH ACTIVITY. REGION 5125 STILL
PROVIDES A CHANCE OF BOTH A PROTON PRODUCING FLARE AND HIGH
ACTIVITY DUE TO ITS APPARENT REFORMATION OF FILAMENTS LINKED
THROUGHOUT THE REGION.

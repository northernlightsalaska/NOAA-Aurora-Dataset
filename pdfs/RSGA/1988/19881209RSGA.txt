IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 08/2100Z
TO 09/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 5265 (N19E08)
PRODUCED NEARLY ALL OF THE FLARES OBSERVED DURING THE INTERVAL,
AMONG THEM AN M2/1N AT 0320Z AND AN M3/0N AT 0737Z. BOTH OF
THOSE IMPULSIVE EVENTS WERE ACCOMPANIED BY MODEST DISCRETE
FREQUENCY RADIO BURSTS. THE H-ALPHA FLARES WERE LARGELY CONFINED
TO THE PLAGE IMMEDIATELY WEST OF THE MAIN SPOT, PERHAPS THE
SITE OF AN ISOLATED POLE. ELSEWHERE, TWO NEW REGIONS WERE
ASSIGNED: 5272 (S15E63) AND 5273 (S33E72). MINOR SURGING WAS
REPORTED AT BOTH LIMBS.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. REGION 5265 SHOULD CONTINUE TO GENERATE M-CLASS
ACTIVITY.

CONDITIONS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE QUIET TO UNSETTLED THROUGHOUT THE FORECAST

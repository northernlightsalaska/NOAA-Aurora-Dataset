ELECTRON FLUXES AT GEOSYNCHRONOUS ALTITUDE WERE HIGH.

SHOULD BE QUIET TO SLIGHTLY UNSETTLED FOR 23-24 JUN. LATE ON
25 JUN, A CORONAL HOLE RELATED DISTURBANCE IS FORECAST TO
BEGIN. ONCE THE HIGH SPEED STREAM IMPACTS THE EARTH, ACTIVE TO
MINOR STORM CONDITIONS ARE EXPECTED.

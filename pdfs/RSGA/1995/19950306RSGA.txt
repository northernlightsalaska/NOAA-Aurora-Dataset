EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY QUIET TO UNSETTLED FOR THE NEXT TWO
DAYS.  BY DAY THREE, THE FIELD IS EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY
UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE IN RESPONSE TO A RECURRENT CORONAL HOLE

UNSETTLED TO MAJOR STORM AT HIGH LATITUDES FOR THE NEXT 12 TO
24 HOURS. ACTIVITY IS THEN EXPECTED TO TAPER OFF TO MOSTLY
UNSETTLED FOR THE REMAINDER OF THE FORECAST PERIOD.

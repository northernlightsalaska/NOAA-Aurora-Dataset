SDF NUMBER 159 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 08 JUN 1995
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 07/2100Z

REGION 7877 (N09W00) GREW IN H-ALPHA AND PRODUCED SEVERAL

IN RECENT HOURS. IT IS NOT MAGNETICALLY COMPLEX. ACTIVE

DEPARTING REGION 7876 (N12).

IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 07/2100Z TO 08/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN QUIET FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS.
THE GREATER THAN 2 MEV ELECTRON FLUXES CONTINUE AT HIGH
LEVELS AT GEOSYNCHRONOUS ALTITUDES. THE DAILY ENERGETIC
ELECTRON FLUENCE REMAINS MODERATE.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO PERSIST AT QUIET LEVELS TOMORROW, BUT BECOME
ACTIVE BY THE END OF THE FORECAST PERIOD IN RESPONSE TO A
POSSIBLE INTERPLANETARY DISTURBANCE ASSOCIATED WITH THE
FILAMENT DISAPPEARANCE OF 7 JUNE.

THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT UNSETTLED TO SEVERE STORM
LEVELS FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS.  ACTIVITY IS BELIEVED TO
EMANATE FROM A FAVORABLY POSITIONED RECURRENT CORONAL HOLE
FEATURE.  THE GREATER THAN 2 MEV ENERGETIC ELECTRON FLUX
AT GEOSYNCHRONOUS ALTITUDE WAS MOSTLY MODERATE TO HIGH.  A
STEADY RISE TO VERY HIGH WAS EVIDENT AFTER 05/2000UT.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE FOR THE NEXT THREE
DAYS.

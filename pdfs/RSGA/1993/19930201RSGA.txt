BEEN DUE TO CORONAL HOLE EFFECTS.

EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY UNSETTLED. BRIEF PERIODS OF ACTIVE
LEVELS ARE POSSIBLE DURING THE FIRST DAY, ESPECIALLY AT HIGH

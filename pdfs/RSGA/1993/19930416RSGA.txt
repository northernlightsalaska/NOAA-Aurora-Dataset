MINOR STORM LEVELS. STORMING OCCURRED FROM 16/0600-1200Z.

EXPECTED TO BE AT ACTIVE LEVELS DURING THE FIRST TWO DAYS
DECLINING TO MOSTLY UNSETTLED LEVELS DURING THE FINAL DAY.

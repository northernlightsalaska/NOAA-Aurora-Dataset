IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 07/2100Z
TO 08/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. REGION 7420 (S07E26),

A C7/1B FLARE AT 07/1715UT AND TWO OTHER SMALL CLASS C FLARES.
THE ONLY OTHER CLASS C ACTIVITY WAS UNCORRELATED. REGION 7417
(N13W06) CONTINUED TO DECAY SLOWLY.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE. REGIONS 7420 AND 7417 BOTH HAVE A GOOD CHANCE
OF M-CLASS ACTIVITY AND ISOLATED X-CLASS ACTIVITY.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 07/2100Z TO 08/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT UNSETTLED TO MINOR STORM
LEVELS. HIGH LATITUDE STATIONS SAW A BRIEF PERIOD OF MAJOR
TO SEVERE STORM LEVELS AT ABOUT 08/1800UT.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE ACTIVE OWING TO FLARE EFFECTS FOR THE NEXT 24
HOURS. THE FIELD IS EXPECTED TO SETTLE DOWN TO MOSTLY UNSETTLED
LEVELS AFTER THAT.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 04/2100Z
TO 05/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE.  A CLASS M1 FLARE
BEGINNING AT 05/0447 UT, WITH NO OPTICAL COUNTERPOINT, WAS
THE HIGHLIGHT OF THE DAY.  REGION 7418 (N05W15) HAS MATURED
WITH CONTINUED GROWTH AND SPOTS FILLING IN THE INTERMEDIATE
PORTION OF THE REGION.  REGION 7420 IS NOW CLEARLY VISIBLE
AT S08E65 AND SHOWS A FULLY DEVELOPED TRAILER. REGION 7419
(N11E39) SHOWS A TREND TOWARD INCREASING COMLEXITY WITH SOME
OBSERVATORIES REPORTING A BETA-GAMMA CONFIGURATION.  ALL THREE
REGIONS APPEAR TO BE MOVING IN A COMMON TREND TOWARD INCREASING
ACTIVITY THOUGH NONE HAS YET PRODUCED A VERIFIED FLARE LARGER
THAN CLASS C.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE TO HIGH WITH PROBABLE CLASS M FLARES AND A CHANCE
OF CLASS X EVENTS.  ANY OF THE THREE REGIONS LISTED ABOVE MAY
CONTRIBUTE TO THE ACTIVITY.

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 11/2100Z
TO 12/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS AT LOW LEVELS. REGION 7500
(N18W24) REMAINS THE LARGEST REGION ON THE DISK, BUT IT IS
STARTING TO DECREASE IN AREA. DURING THIS FORECAST PERIOD

C -CLASS FLARES. REGION 7496'S (N17W45) MAGNETIC

IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 12/2100Z

UNCORRELATED B-CLASS X-RAY BURST OCCURRED. NEW REGION 7580
(N07W16) WAS NUMBERED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
VERY LOW.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 12/2100Z TO 13/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT ACTIVE TO MAJOR STORM
LEVELS FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS AT MIDDLE LATITUDES. ACTIVITY AT
HIGHER LATITUDES RANGED FROM ACTIVE TO SEVERE STORM LEVELS.
STORMING AT ALL LATITUDES COMMENCED NEAR 13/0000Z. ACTIVITY
DECLINED TO MOSTLY ACTIVE LEVELS BY NEAR 13/1500Z. A PROPERLY
POSITIONED CORONAL WAS PROBABLY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE STORMING.
VERY BRIGHT AURORAE WERE OBSERVED OVER SEATTLE, WASHINGTON,
USA FROM APPROXIMATELY 13/0630-0930Z AS A RESULT OF THE
STORMING.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE AT ACTIVE TO MINOR STORM LEVELS DURING THE
FIRST DAY AND A HALF DUE TO CONTINUING CORONAL HOLE EFFECTS.
THE FIELD IS EXPECTED TO DECLINE TO UNSETTLED LEVELS FOR THE
BALANCE OF THE PERIOD.

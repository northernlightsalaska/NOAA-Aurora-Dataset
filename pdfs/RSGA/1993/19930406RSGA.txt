GAMMA CONFIGURATION. REGION 7469 (S11E02) ALSO PRODUCED SEVERAL

IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE

FAIR CHANCE FOR AN ISOLATED M-CLASS EVENT FROM ONE OF THESE
TWO GROUPS. BACKGROUND LEVELS ARE EXPECTED TO INCREASE WITH
THE RETURN OF OLD REGION 7448 TOMORROW (N17, L-350) WHICH
PRODUCED 4 M-CLASS FLARES ON ITS PREVIOUS DISK TRANSIT.

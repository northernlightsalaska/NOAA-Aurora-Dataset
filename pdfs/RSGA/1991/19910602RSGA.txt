JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 153 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 02 JUN 1991
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 01/2100Z
TO 02/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS HIGH. REGION 6659 (N26E85) PRO-
DUCED AN M5 FLARE AT 0746Z WHICH WAS ACCOMPANIED BY MODEST DIS-
CRETE RADIO BURSTS. THIS REGION HAS NOT YET FULLY ROTATED INTO
VIEW, BUT CURRENT OBSERVATIONS REPORTED STRONG SURGING AND THAT
A VERY LARGE PENUMBRAL MASS IS ROTATING INTO VIEW. REGION 6652
(S09W20) PRODUCED A LONG DURATION M2/2N WHICH PEAKED AT 1426Z.
THE EVENT WAS ACCOMPANIED BY A GROUP OF MODERATE TO OUTSTANDING
DISCRETE RADIO BURSTS AND A STRONG, LONG DURATION TYPE IV SWEEP.
THE GROUP PRODUCED AN ADDITIONAL M1/1N AT 2033Z. THE REGION
HAS A SIMPLE DELTA CONFIGURATION AND IS SHOWING SIGNS OF SLIGHT
DECAY. AN ADDITIONAL UNCORRELATED M1 FLARE OCCURRED AT 0041Z.
REGION 6654 (N07W15) SHOWED CONSIDERABLE DECAY AND SIMPLIFICA-
TION TODAY AND WAS FLARE QUIET. REGION 6648 (S13W41) CONTINUES
TO GROW STEADILY AS DOES REGION 6658 (N20E51).
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE TO HIGH. THE PRINCIPAL THREATS FOR MAJOR FLARES APPEAR
AT THIS TIME TO BE REGIONS 6659 AND 6652. REGIONS 6648, 6654,
AND 6658 MIGHT CONTRIBUTE M-CLASS LEVEL FLARE ACTIVITY AS WELL.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 01/2100Z TO 02/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD RANGED FROM ACTIVE TO MINOR STORM LEVELS
DURING THE PERIOD WITH SOME BRIEF PERIODS OF SEVERE STORM
LEVELS AT HIGH LATITUDES. THE MOST DISTURBED PERIOD DURING
THE DAY WAS FROM 0300-0900Z. A GREATER THAN 10 MEV PROTON
EVENT CONTINUES IN PROGRESS, ALTHOUGH THE FLUX LEVELS DID
DROP BELOW THRESHOLD OCCASIONALLY. THE PEAK LEVELS OBSERVED
TODAY WERE AT 18 PFU AT 0715Z AND AT 2030Z.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE AT ACTIVE TO MINOR STORM LEVELS FOR THE NEXT 24
HOURS AS THE CURRENT DISTURBANCE PERSISTS. CONDITIONS ARE EX-
PECTED TO DECLINE GRADUALLY OVER THE NEXT FEW DAYS: UNSETTLED
TO ACTIVE WITH HIGH LATITUDE MINOR STORMING IS EXPECTED ON
DAY 2, AND UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE LEVELS BY DAY 3.

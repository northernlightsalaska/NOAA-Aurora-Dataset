IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 10/2100Z
TO 11/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE.  THERE WAS AN M3
X-RAY FLARE AT 2110Z - OPTICAL OBSERVATIONS WERE NOT AVAILABLE.
REGION 6824 (S21E31) AND REGION 6827 (S20E49) PRODUCED A FEW
ADDITIONAL SUB-FLARES.  NEW REGION 6828 (S11E65) WAS NUMBERED.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE.  REGIONS 6824 AND 6827 HAVE BOTH BECOME
SOMEWHAT MORE COMPLEX MAGNETICALLY, AND EITHER MAY PRODUCE AN
ISOLATED M-FLARE.  REGION 6823 (N03W22) ALSO HAS SOME M-FLARE
POTENTIAL.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 10/2100Z TO 11/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS INITIALLY AT QUIET TO UNSETTLED
LEVELS, INCREASED BRIEFLY TO ACTIVE TO MINOR STORM LEVELS
FROM 0900-1500Z, AND ENDED THE PERIOD WITH UNSETTLED TO
ACTIVE CONDITIONS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY ACTIVE THROUGH THE PERIOD, WITH
PERIODS OF MINOR STORM LEVELS AT HIGH LATITUDES FOR THE
FIRST TWO DAYS.

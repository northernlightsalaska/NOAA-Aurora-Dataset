JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 299 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 26 OCT 1991
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 25/2100Z
TO 26/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS HIGH. REGION 6891 (S20E21)
JUST GENERATED AN X1/2N TENFLARE WITH MAXIMUM AT 2047Z. THIS
EVENT WAS SLOW TO DEVELOP, HAVING BEGUN AT 1846Z AND
REACHING WHAT SEEMED TO BE A LENGTHY MAXIMUM AT THE M1 LEVEL
BEFORE THE SECOND INCREASE BEGAN ABOUT 2020Z. PRELIMINARY
REPORTS OF RADIO BURSTS INCLUDE 1200 FLUX UNITS AT 2695 MHZ. NO
SWEEPS HAVE BEEN REPORTED AS YET. OTHER SIGNIFICANT ACTIVITY
FROM THIS REGION INCLUDED A LONG-DURATION M1/0F AT 0015Z. THIS
EVENT LASTED ABOUT 4 HOURS AND INCLUDED A TYPE IV OF DURATION
203 MINUTES. ITS OTHER ENERGETIC FLARE WAS AN M1/1F AT 1301Z.
REGION 6891 EXPERIENCED CONSIDERABLE FRAGMENTATION OF ITS
LARGE LEADER PENUMBRA OVER THE PAST 24 HOURS, BUT CLEARLY
REMAINS POTENT. ELSEWHERE, TWO NEW REGIONS WERE ASSIGNED, 6895
(N24W31) AND 6896 (S03E63).
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE TO HIGH. REGION 6891 SHOWS NO SIGNS OF ABATING ITS
PRODUCTION OF M AND X CLASS FLARES.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 25/2100Z TO 26/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS AT MOSTLY ACTIVE LEVELS THE PAST
24 HOURS. A SUDDEN IMPULSE MEASURING APPROXIMATELY 43 NANO-
TELA AT BOULDER OCCURRED AT 0635Z.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE THROUGHOUT THE FORECAST
PERIOD.

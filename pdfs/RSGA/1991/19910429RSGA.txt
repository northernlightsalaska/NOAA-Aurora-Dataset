1852Z, WAS CORRELATED WITH ACTIVITY ON THE DISK. REGION 6603
(S29W75) SHOWED SOME DECAY WHILE REMAINING GENERALLY QUIET.

IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW. ONLY A SLIGHT CHANCE FOR M-CLASS FLARING EXISTS.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 28/2100Z TO 29/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT ACTIVE TO MAJOR STORM
LEVELS FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS IN RESPONSE TO EFFECTS FROM A
CORONAL HOLE. AURORAL ACTIVITY HAS BEEN OBSERVED IN THE
SOUTHERN HEMISPHERE BY THE ORBITER DISCOVERY CREW DURING THE
LAST 24 HOURS.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO CONTINUE AT STORM LEVELS FOR THE NEXT 24-36 HOURS
DECLINING TO UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE CONDITIONS BY THE END OF
THE PERIOD.

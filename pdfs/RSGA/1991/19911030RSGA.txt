JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 303 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 30 OCT 1991
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 29/2100Z
TO 30/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS HIGH. REGION 6891 (S12W32)
PRODUCED A LONG DURATION X2/3B TENFLARE AT 0634Z. STRONG
RADIO BURSTS ACROSS THE SPECTRUM, TYPE II AND IV RADIO SWEEPS
AND A 5200 SFU TENFLARE WERE REPORTED WITH THIS FLARE. THE
SHOCK SPEED FROM THE TYPE II SWEEP WAS ESTIMATED AT 1600 KM/SEC.
PRIOR TO THIS X FLARE REGION 6891 PRODUCED AN M1/SN AT 2142Z.
AN IMPULSIVE M4/1B AT 1916Z ROUNDED OUT TODAYS ACTIVITY FROM
6891. THE REGION APPEARS TO HAVE BEGUN A FRAGMENTATION OF THE
LARGE TRAILER SPOT, PERHAPS SIGNALING THE BEGINNING OF ITS
DECAY PROCESS. OTHER ACTIVITY TODAY INCLUDED A C9/2F FROM
REGION 6892 (S19W33) AT 0435Z.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE TO HIGH. REGION 6891 REMAINS THE MAIN THREAT FOR
CONTINUED ACTIVITY. REGION 6893 (N71W11) MAY ADD ADDITIONAL
MODERATE ACTIVITY IF THE GROWTH REPORTED OVERNIGHT CONTINUES.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 29/2100Z TO 30/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT QUIET TO ACTIVE LEVELS
FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS. THE STORMING REPORTED YESTERDAY
STOPPED ABRUPTLY AT THE BEGINNING OF THE UT DAY, WITH
PERIODS OF QUIET CONDITIONS AT MID-LATITUDES BEING REPORTED.
BY 1200Z CONDITIONS WERE MOSTLY ACTIVE AGAIN. A SATELLITE
LEVEL PROTON EVENT BEGAN AT 0745Z IN RESPONSE TO THE EARLIER
X2. THE EVENT REACHED A MAXIMUM OF 94 PFU AT THE GREATER THAN
10MEV LEVEL AT 0810Z AND IS AT THE EVENT THRESHOLD OF 10 PFU
AT GREATER THAN 10 MEV AT THE TIME OF THIS WRITING. A PCA
EVENT WAS ENDED AT 1600Z.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BEGIN THE PERIOD AT MOSTLY ACTIVE CONDITIONS. A
SHOCK FROM THE X2 IS ANTICIPATED BETWEEN 1200 AND 1800Z ON 31
OCTOBER. IF THIS SHOCK MATERIALIZES, MINOR TO MAJOR STORMING
CAN BE EXPECTED FOR 24 TO 36 HOURS THEREAFTER. THE PROTON
EVENT NOW IN PROGRESS IS EXPECTED TO END SHORTLY BUT MAY
CROSS BACK ABOVE THE EVENT THRESHOLD IF AND WHEN THE
ANTICIPATED SHOCK ARRIVES.

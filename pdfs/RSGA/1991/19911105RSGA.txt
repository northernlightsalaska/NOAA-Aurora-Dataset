SDF NUMBER 309 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 05 NOV 1991
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 04/2100Z
TO 05/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE DUE TO AN M3 X-RAY
FLARE AT 04/2323Z. REGION 6906 (S16W16) IS EMERGING RAPIDLY,
SHOWING CONSIDERABLE PENUMBRAL DEVELOPMENT SINCE YESTERDAY.
THIS REGION HAS PRODUCED SEVERAL C-CLASS SUBFLARES. ACTIVITY
AT THE EAST LIMB NEAR NE24 DURING THE PAST 36 HOURS HAS
INCLUDED AN EPL AT 04/1150Z, AN EPL AT 04/2346Z AND A SPRAY
AT 05/1547Z, SUGGESTING THAT A SIGNIFICANT NEW ACTIVE REGION
MAY SOON ROTATE INTO VIEW. THE EPL AT 04/2346Z MAY HAVE
CONTRIBUTED TO THE M3 FLARE MENTIONED ABOVE. THE FOLLOWING NEW
REGIONS WERE NUMBERED - 6913 (S32W18), 6914 (S17W05), 6915
(N16E64), AND 6916 (S23E66).
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE. AN M-CLASS FLARE IS POSSIBLE IN REGION 6906.

IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS

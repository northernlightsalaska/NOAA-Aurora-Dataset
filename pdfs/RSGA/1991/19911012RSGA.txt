IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 11/2100Z

UNCORRELATED M2 X-RAY FLARE AT 12/1008Z. REGION 6873 (S24E22)
CONTINUED TO PRODUCE C-CLASS FLARES, THE LARGEST THIS PAST
DAY BEING A C9/SF AT 12/0507Z. THIS REGION HAS SHOWN SOME

THIS REGION CONTINUES TO PRODUCE SMALL SUBFLARES WITHOUT
SIGNIFICANT X-RAY OUTPUT.

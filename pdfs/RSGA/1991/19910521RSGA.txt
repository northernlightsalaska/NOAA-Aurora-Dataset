IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 20/2100Z

NOTE TODAY WAS FROM A REGION JUST EMERGING ON THE EAST LIMB
AT N22. THIS AREA PRODUCED A C5/1N FLARE AT 0955Z AND A
C2/SF FLARE AT 1652Z. A HOST OF SMALL SUBFLARES WERE ALSO

ASSIGNED.

CONDITIONS BECAME PREDOMINANT. THE PROTON FLUX AT GREATER
THAN 10 MEV REMAINED SLIGHTLY ENHANCED THROUGHOUT THE PERIOD.

EXPECTED TO BE GENERALLY UNSETTLED UNTIL 24 MAY WHEN ACTIVE
CONDITIONS ARE POSSIBLE IN RESPONSE TO EFFECTS FROM A CORONAL

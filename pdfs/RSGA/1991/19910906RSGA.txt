JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 249 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 06 SEP 1991
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 05/2100Z
TO 06/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 6811 (S21W55)
BEGAN THE INTERVAL WITH AN M2/1N TENFLARE AT 05/2207Z, AND HAS
HAD A NUMBER OF SMALLER FLARES SINCE. YESTERDAY'S HIGH ACHIEVER,
REGION 6805 (N23W90), SHOWED BUT ONE SUBFLARE AND OCCASIONAL
SURGING AS IT BEGAN WEST LIMB PASSAGE. REGION 6818 (S12W39)
CONTINUED TO GROW AND PRODUCED TWO MID-SIZED C-CLASS FLARES.
NEWLY ASSIGNED REGION 6820 (S11E28) WAS BORN ON THE DISK.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW TO MODERATE. REGIONS 6805 AND 6811 MAY GENERATE AN
ISOLATED ENERGETIC EVENT AT SOME POINT IN THE NEXT THREE DAYS.
REGION 6818, IF IT CONTINUES TO DEVELOP, MAY ALSO CONTRIBUTE.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 05/2100Z TO 06/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT MOSTLY UNSETTLED LEVELS. AN
ENHANCEMENT IN THE GREATER THAN 10 MEV PROTON FLUX BEGAN
AROUND 0800Z. THE CURRENT VALUE IS 2 PFU. THE SOURCE OF THE
ENHANCEMENT DOES NOT SEEM TO BE FLARE-RELATED.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE. THERE IS A CHANCE FOR
EVEN HIGHER LEVELS OF ACTIVITY NEAR THE END OF THE PERIOD
DUE TO EFFECTS FROM THE FILAMENT DISAPPEARANCES OF 04-05 SEPT.
THE CURRENT PROTON ENHANCEMENT IS LIKELY TO WANE IN THE NEXT
24 HOURS.

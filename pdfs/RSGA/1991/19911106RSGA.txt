IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 05/2100Z
TO 06/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. FOUR M-CLASS FLARES
OCCURRED DURING THIS REPORTING PERIOD. THREE OF THE FOUR
M-FLARES OCCURRED IN REGION 6906 (S16W30). THE LARGEST OF THESE
WAS A M4/SB FLARE AT 0450Z. REGION 6906 CONTINUES TO SHOW GROWTH
IN BOTH LEADER AND TRAILER SPOTS, BUT REMAINS MAGNETICALLY
SIMPLE. REMAINING SPOT GROUPS ARE SMALL AND UNIMPRESSIVE. A 24
DEGREE LONG FILAMENT DISAPPEARED OVERNIGHT IN THE SE QUADRANT.
IT WAS LAST SEEN AT 2022Z AND CENTERED AT S32E32.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. REGION 6906 SHOULD BE THE PRODUCER OF M-CLASS
ACTIVITY.

IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS

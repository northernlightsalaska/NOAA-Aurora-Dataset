JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 195 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 14 JUL 1991
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 13/2100Z
TO 14/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. REGION 6718 (S24W26)
DISPLAYED LITTLE CHANGE DURING THE PERIOD. IT REMAINS SOMEWHAT
COMPLEX WITH ITS DELTA MAGNETIC CONFIGURATION. TWO M-CLASS
EVENTS WERE ASSOCIATED WITH THIS AREA: AN M1/1N FLARE AT 0330Z
AND AN M1/SN FLARE AT 0736Z. BOTH EVENTS WERE ACCOMPANIED BY
WEAK RADIO EMISSIONS. SEVERAL C-CLASS EVENTS WERE ALSO OBSERVED
FROM THIS REGION. AN M1/1N FLARE OCCURRED IN REGION 6722
(S15E01) AT 0350Z. THIS REGION SHOWED SLIGHT DECAY BUT REMAINS
FAIRLY COMPLEX. OTHER ACTIVITY OF NOTE INCLUDES ISOLATED
FLARING IN REGIONS 6713 (N35W80) AND 6728 (S26E50) AND AN
ERUPTIVE PROMINENCE NOTED FROM REGION 6729 (S21E72) AT 0657Z.
NO NEW REGIONS WERE ASSIGNED TODAY.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. ISOLATED M-CLASS FLARING IS POSSIBLE FROM REGIONS
6718 AND 6722.

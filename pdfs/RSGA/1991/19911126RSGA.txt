SDF NUMBER 330 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 26 NOV 1991
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 25/2100Z
TO 26/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS LOW. TODAY'S ACTIVITY CONSISTED
OF ONLY A FEW LOW LEVEL C-CLASS FLARES. MOST OF THESE WERE PRO-
DUCED BY EITHER REGION 6940 (S12E18) OR REGION 6943 (S06E38).
THESE TWO REGIONS SHOWED STEADY GROWTH DURING THE PERIOD, AC-
COUNTING FOR TODAY'S INCREASE OF 10.7 CM SOLAR FLUX.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
LOW. ONLY C-CLASS FLARES ARE EXPECTED DURING THE FORECAST
PERIOD.
IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 25/2100Z TO 26/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD WAS QUIET TO UNSETTLED.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE GENERALLY UNSETTLED UNTIL SOMETIME LATE TO-
MORROW WHEN ACTIVE CONDITIONS ARE EXPECTED IN RESPONSE TO
A FAVORABLY POSITIONED CORONAL HOLE. ACTIVE CONDITIONS SHOULD
PERSIST THROUGH THE SECOND DAY AND PARTWAY THROUGH DAY THREE.
CONDITIONS ARE EXPECTED TO DECLINE GRADUALLY BY THE END OF
DAY THREE.

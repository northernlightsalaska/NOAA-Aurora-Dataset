JOINT USAF/NOAA REPORT OF SOLAR AND GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY
SDF NUMBER 070 ISSUED AT 2200Z ON 11 MAR 1991
IA.  ANALYSIS OF SOLAR ACTIVE REGIONS AND ACTIVITY FROM 10/2100Z
TO 11/2100Z:  SOLAR ACTIVITY WAS MODERATE. AN M3 X-RAY EVENT
ERUPTED AT 0734Z. OPTICAL OBSERVATIONS WERE VERY LIMITED AT THE
ONSET OF THIS ACTIVITY SO NO FLARE WAS OBSERVED, HOWEVER POST
FLARE LOOPS WERE NOTED IN REGION 6545 (S09E62) SHORTLY AFTER
INDICATING THE X-RAY EVENT MAY HAVE BEEN GENERATED BY THIS
AREA. A C4/SF FLARE WAS NOTED IN 6545 AT 1040Z. REGION 6538
(S23E07) APPEARS TO HAVE REACHED ITS PEAK IN GROWTH AND IS
BEGINNING TO SIMPLIFY MAGNETICALLY. SEVERAL C-CLASS FLARES WERE
GENERATED BY THIS AREA, THE LARGEST OF WHICH WAS A C5/SF FLARE
AT 10/2244Z. NEW REGION 6546 (S22E75) WAS ASSIGNED TODAY.
IB.  SOLAR ACTIVITY FORECAST:  SOLAR ACTIVITY IS EXPECTED TO BE
MODERATE. REGION 6538 OR 6545 MAY PRODUCE ISOLATED M-CLASS
FLARING.

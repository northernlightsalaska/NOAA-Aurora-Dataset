IIA.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY SUMMARY FROM 01/2100Z TO 02/2100Z:
THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD HAS BEEN AT ACTIVE TO MAJOR STORM
LEVELS FOR THE PAST 24 HOURS. THE STORMING BEGAN TO SUBSIDE
SHORTLY AFTER 02/1800Z. THIS STORMING WAS PROBABLY IN
RESPONSE TO THE X2/2B OF 31/0052Z.
IIB.  GEOPHYSICAL ACTIVITY FORECAST:  THE GEOMAGNETIC FIELD IS
EXPECTED TO BE MOSTLY ACTIVE TOMORROW WITH PERIODS OF MINOR
STORMING POSSIBLE AT HIGH LATITUDES. UNSETTLED TO ACTIVE
LEVELS WITH SOME HIGH LATITUDE MINOR STORMING IS EXPECTED ON
THE SECOND DAY. ACTIVE TO MINOR STORMING IS EXPECTED ON THE
FINAL DAY. THE SECOND AND THIRD DAY STORMING IS EXPECTED IN
RESPONSE TO A FAVORABLY POSITIONED CORONAL HOLE.
